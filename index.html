Our Story

From Nadhi Sign to UV SIGN

Started in 2003 as "Nadhi Sign", we were the pioneers of modern signage in Mukkam, Calicut. Over the last two decades, we have evolved with technology, rebranding to UV SIGN to reflect our commitment to cutting-edge UV printing solutions.

With a skilled team and state-of-the-art machinery, we handle everything from large-scale hoardings to intricate corporate gifts with consistent quality and speed.
